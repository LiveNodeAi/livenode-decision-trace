AI can give us an answer in seconds. LiveNode Decision Trace preserves why a decision was made.

I paste one fictional meeting transcript. The public demo accepts up to thirty thousand characters.

A low-cost model detects two decision topics and grounds each topic in exact transcript segments.

Before generation, the user can review, rename, select, or exclude each topic.

Each selected topic becomes a Decision Trace: situation, assumptions, criteria, trade-offs, recommendation, and next actions.

Grounded evidence stays separate from AI inference, so a polished answer cannot silently become a supplied fact.

The browser creates a Markdown ZIP with a meeting summary, actions, Decision Traces, and reusable KX Notes.

Codex built the demo. GPT-5.6 structures each reviewed decision.
